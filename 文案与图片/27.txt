FIOSIG Coffee Store Design
The second FIOSIG Coffee store, which opened on May 4, 2021, is a brand - new space designed by Volcanic Konan Design.
In fact, we least like to limit design by style. So, we would just say that this is a space that suits FIOSIG. Space, color, and lighting are all designed to present a warm and attentive community store.
Friends who often visit FIOSIG Coffee will surely feel that the new store is a "comprehensive upgrade in terms of function, space, and image" compared to the old one when they come to the new store.
However, in terms of the visual experience, we have captured and transformed many experiences from the old store and presented them in a new form in this space.
【Yes! It's still the familiar FIOSIG, but with a comprehensive upgrade!This is all we've done for FIOSIG and its customers who love it.】
