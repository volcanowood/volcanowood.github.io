The Ten Ounce Books & Coffee Studio located on Tianfu 4th Street has completed its upgrade. We define this place as "Ten Ounce Books & Coffee Studio".
The combined presentation of the coffee space and the bookstore is an interpretation of coffee and books, two daily necessities. Here, you can enjoy a more immersive coffee - tasting experience and find wonderful books carefully selected by the owner. Everyone is welcome to come here, take a seat, read, and savor.
